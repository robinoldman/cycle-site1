Bad Kleinkircheim Route

Difficulty: Hard
Distance: 10k

The Bad Kleinkircheim route invites cyclists to embark on an extraordinary journey through the enchanting alpine landscape. This remarkable cycling route unveils a tapestry of breathtaking beauty, where rugged mountains soar majestically, verdant valleys stretch as far as the eye can see, and crystal-clear lakes glisten under the golden sun.

The terrain presents a thrilling mix of challenges and rewards, with invigorating uphill climbs that push your limits and exhilarating descents that set your heart racing.
