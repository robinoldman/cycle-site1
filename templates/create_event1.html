Millstatt Route

Difficulty: Easy
Distance: 10k

The Millstatt route offers a mesmerising journey through picturesque alpine landscapes, immersing cyclists in the breathtaking beauty of the terrain. This cycling route showcases a harmonious blend of towering mountains, lush valleys, and serene lakes, creating a captivating backdrop at every turn.

As you pedal along the route, you'll be enchanted by the pristine nature surrounding you, with vibrant meadows, dense forests, and crystal-clear streams.
